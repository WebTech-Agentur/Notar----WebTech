Professional Regulations

The relevant professional regulations for notaries apply. These include, in particular:

  • Federal Notary Act (Bundesnotarordnung - BNotO)
  • Notarization Act (Beurkundungsgesetz - BeurkG)
  • Guidelines of the Notary Chambers (Richtlinien der Notarkammern)
  • Official Regulations for Notaries (Dienstordnung für Notarinnen und Notare - DONot)
  • Court and Notary Costs Act (Gerichts- und Notarkostengesetz - GNotKG)

These regulations are accessible via the Federal Chamber of Notaries website at www.bnotk.de in the section "Berufsrecht".
