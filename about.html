About Us

Expertise, tradition, and a commitment to absolute legal certainty.

Dr. Maximilian Schmidt

Notary Public

Professional Philosophy

As an independent bearer of a public office, my foremost duty is to ensure legal certainty and to protect inexperienced parties. My philosophy is to translate complex legal regulations into clear, understandable language for my clients.

Trust is the foundation of every notarial act. I take the time to deeply understand your individual situation, whether it involves a complex corporate restructuring or a sensitive family matter. My goal is to craft precise, legally binding documents that stand the test of time and prevent future disputes.

Qualifications & Background

With over 15 years of experience in the legal field, I bring a wealth of practical and theoretical knowledge to every consultation.

  • Legal Studies at the University of Bonn and LMU Munich
  • Doctorate in Corporate Law (Dr. iur.)
  • Appointment as Notary Public in Wesseling
  • Extensive experience in inheritance and real estate law
