Our Services

We provide specialized notarial services designed to offer you the highest level of legal security and peace of mind.

Real Estate Law

Real estate transactions are often the most significant financial decisions in a person's life. We ensure that your interests are fully protected whether you are buying your first home, selling commercial property, or transferring real estate within the family.

We handle all aspects of the transaction, ensuring clear titles, verifying land register entries, and managing the escrow accounts securely.

  • Real Estate Purchase Agreements (Residential and Commercial)
  • Creation and Deletion of Land Charges (Grundschulden) & Mortgages
  • Property Transfers and Gifts within Families
  • Condominium Declarations (Teilungserklärungen)
  • Usufruct and Residential Rights (Nießbrauch & Wohnrecht)

Corporate Law

Founding and managing a company requires a solid legal framework. We advise founders, managing directors, and shareholders on all corporate legal matters. From selecting the right legal entity to drafting complex shareholder agreements, we ensure your business operations rest on a legally secure foundation.

We also act as the crucial interface to the commercial register (Handelsregister) for all required filings.

  • Company Formations (GmbH, UG, AG, GmbH & Co. KG)
  • Commercial Register Applications & Amendments
  • Share Transfers and Capital Increases
  • Corporate Restructuring & Mergers
  • Appointment and Dismissal of Managing Directors

Family Law

Family law matters require intense empathy combined with precise legal structuring. Statutory regulations often do not align with individual life plans. We help you create customized, fair agreements that provide clarity and prevent future emotional and financial disputes during difficult times.

  • Drafting of Marriage Contracts (Eheverträge)
  • Divorce and Separation Agreements (Trennungs- und Scheidungsfolgenvereinbarungen)
  • Adoptions (Stepchild and Adult Adoptions)
  • Partnership Agreements for unmarried couples

Inheritance Law

Properly planning your succession ensures that your wishes are respected and your loved ones are protected from legal conflicts and unforeseen tax burdens. A notarial will or inheritance contract is legally binding, cannot be lost, and often replaces the need for an expensive certificate of inheritance (Erbschein).

We also strongly advise on precautionary measures for cases of incapacity.

  • Wills and Inheritance Contracts (Testamente und Erbverträge)
  • Anticipated Succession (Vorweggenommene Erbfolge) & Gifts
  • Precautionary Powers of Attorney (Vorsorgevollmachten)
  • Living Wills / Advance Directives (Patientenverfügungen)
  • Applications for Certificates of Inheritance
